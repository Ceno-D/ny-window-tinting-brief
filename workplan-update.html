The Situation

What Happened
in June

⚠️
8-Day Data Gap — June 12–20, 2026

In mid-June, the third-party platform (GoHighLevel) hosting the landing page was unexpectedly paused, leaving ad clicks with no destination for approximately 8 days. During this window, ad spend continued but leads were not being captured. Any conversion data from June 12–20 is unreliable.

Rather than re-engage with the third-party dependency, a decision was made to build a purpose-built landing page from scratch — one designed specifically to align with the ad campaigns running. The new page launched June 20, 2026 and is live at windowtint.newyorkwindowtinting.com.

This was the right call. The old page had 11 H1 tags, no visible reviews widget, and was not built to convert paid ad traffic. The new page was designed around the exact keywords the campaigns target — headline copy, service descriptions, CTAs, and trust signals are all aligned to the running ads.

✅
Clean Data Collection Starts Now

As of June 23, all three major updates are live: new landing page (June 20), updated ad copy in the Near Me and Local ad groups (Phase 3 rewrite), and 20 new negative keywords added today eliminating car washes, auto repair shops, and competitor brand searches. The data clock resets from here.
